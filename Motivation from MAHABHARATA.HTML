Key Life Lessons

1. Consequences of Anger and Ego

Duryodhana's refusal to share power due to jealousy and ego leads to destruction.

2. Importance of Righteousness

Yudhishthira's adherence to truth ultimately preserves dharma despite challenges.

3. Complexity of Human Nature

Characters like Karna show that people contain both virtues and flaws.

4. Duty and Detachment

Arjuna learns to perform his duty as a warrior without personal attachment.

5. Impermanence of Power

The epic repeatedly shows that wealth and power are temporary.
